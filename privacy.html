Privacy Policy

How Cynevor handles personal information

Cynevor Group is committed to handling personal information responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable data protection law. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

At a glance

  • We only collect data where there is a clear and lawful purpose
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties
  • We use proportionate safeguards to protect information
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data

Scope

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to visitors to the Cynevor Group website at cynevor.com and to anyone who contacts us regarding services, partnerships, careers, academy interest, or general enquiries.

Information We May Collect

Limited to what is necessary and proportionate

Depending on how you interact with our website, we may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Company or organisation details where relevant
  • The content of enquiries or messages submitted to us
  • Technical data such as browser type, device information, and pages visited
  • Analytics and usage data to understand how the site is used
  • Cookie-related data (see Cookies section below)

We do not seek to collect unnecessary personal information.

Lawful basis for processing

  • Consent — where you have given clear agreement
  • Legitimate interests — in operating and improving our website and responding to genuine enquiries
  • Contractual necessity — where processing is required to fulfil a service
  • Legal obligations — where we are required to process data by law
Use

How we use your information

We use personal information to respond to enquiries, provide services, manage communications, improve the website, and fulfil any legal or contractual obligations. We do not use it for unsolicited marketing.

Sharing

Who we may share data with

Cynevor does not sell personal information. We may share data with carefully selected service providers who support our operations, or where required by law. We do not share data for third-party marketing purposes.

Cookies

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or analytics tools to understand how the site is used and to improve performance. Where analytics are used, they are configured to collect only the minimum data necessary.

Retention

How long we keep data

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or where required by law. Enquiry data is not held indefinitely.

Security

How we protect information

We apply appropriate technical and organisational controls to reduce the risk of unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Access to personal data is limited to those with a genuine operational need.

Your Rights

Rights you hold

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also object to certain processing, withdraw consent where applicable, and request restriction of processing. To exercise any right, please contact us.

Contact Regarding Privacy

Privacy queries and rights requests

If you have a question about this policy or wish to exercise any of your data rights, please contact us at: [contact@cynevor.com]

We will respond promptly and within any applicable legal timeframes.

Policy Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
