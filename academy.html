Cynevor Academy

Practical digital pathways that widen access to opportunity

Cynevor Academy is being developed to create future pathways into coding and digital skills through practical learning, mentoring, and supportive development.

Placeholder for academy imagery, mentoring sessions, or workshop photography.

Why The Academy Matters

Skills should be accessible, relevant, and connected to real futures

Too many people with potential are excluded by cost, confidence barriers, or lack of practical routes into technology. The academy exists to close that gap through structured, human-centred learning experiences.

Core outcomes

  • Confidence in applied digital and coding fundamentals
  • Mentoring that supports progression into real opportunities
  • Pathways that promote social mobility through technology
Future Programmes

Future Programmes
