Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Inclusion is part of Cynevor from day one

DEI is not a policy added later; it is part of how we are designing our culture, pathways, and leadership from the outset.

Core priorities

  • Equal opportunity in hiring, development, progression, and pay
  • Inclusive pathways into technology and leadership
  • Long-term accountability for inclusion outcomes

Our Commitment

Respect, dignity, and fairness for everyone

Cynevor is committed to equal opportunity and equitable treatment across every part of the group. We want people to feel safe, valued, and able to contribute fully, regardless of background, identity, or lived experience.

Inclusion From Day One

Built into structures, not left to chance

We are embedding inclusive practice in role design, hiring processes, onboarding, leadership behaviour, and team development from the earliest stage of growth.

Equal Opportunity

Equal Opportunity In Practice

We will apply fairness in hiring, development, progression, and pay through clear criteria, transparent standards, and regular review.

Better Pathways

Building Better Pathways

We are committed to widening access into technology and leadership for women, LGBT+ individuals, and people from ethnic and culturally diverse backgrounds, including those historically underrepresented in digital sectors.

Support & Belonging

Inclusion Through Culture

Inclusive culture is built through everyday actions: listening, respectful challenge, mentoring, and leadership that is accountable for creating belonging.

Long-term Accountability

Commitments that are reviewed, measured, and improved over time

We will track progress, seek feedback, and adapt where gaps remain. Building inclusion is a continuous practice, and we accept the responsibility to keep learning as we grow.

How we stay accountable

  • Regular review of hiring, development, and progression outcomes
  • Clear leadership responsibility for inclusive team culture
  • Ongoing listening with colleagues and community partners

Help shape inclusive pathways with us

We welcome conversation with educators, community groups, and professionals committed to broader access and fairer outcomes.
